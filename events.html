Past Events

Highlights from our previous activities

March 13, 2025

What to do after my PhD/PostDoc?

We welcomed Audrey Peters from Academic Transfer and Pablo Mosteiro Romero from Utrecht University to share insights on career paths for researchers. The event featured engaging talks followed by pizza and networking with fellow PhD candidates and postdocs working in NLP and Text Mining.

Location: Martinus J. Langeveldgebouw, Room E.314
Time: 16:00 - 18:00

Career Development
October 17, 2024

NLP@UU Young Scholars Network Kick-Off Event

Utrecht University officially launched the NLP@UU Young Scholars Network (YSN). This inaugural event brought together PhDs, Postdocs, and junior researchers in NLP to shape the network's future direction and build our collaborative community.

Location: Martinus J. Langeveldgebouw, E3.14
Address: Heidelberglaan 1, 3584 CS Utrecht
