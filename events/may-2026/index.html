Career Paths Inside & Outside Academia

A conversation for PhDs, postdocs, and early-career NLP researchers

About the event

This meeting explores different career paths inside and outside academia for researchers in NLP and related fields. We will hear two perspectives:

  • Prof. Albert Gatt (Utrecht University) will share insights on the academic career path and what to expect in its later stages.
  • A speaker from University Medical Center Utrecht (UMCU) will introduce career opportunities in medical centers, including applied research and social impact.

After the talks, we will connect and network over pizza.

Program

  • 15:00 – 15:30 Talk by Prof. Albert Gatt
  • 15:30 – 16:00 Talk by UMCU speaker
  • 16:00 – 17:00 Socializing & pizza
