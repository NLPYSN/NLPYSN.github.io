Join Our Community

We invite young NLP scholars at UU and UMCU to join us

The Young Scholar Network is open to all PhDs, Postdocs, and early-career researchers in Natural Language Processing at Utrecht University and UMC Utrecht.

Whether you're just starting your research journey or looking to expand your network, we welcome you to be part of our growing community.
