About YSN

Building a thriving community for the next generation of NLP researchers

What we do

YSN connects PhD candidates, postdocs, and early-career researchers in NLP at Utrecht University and UMCU. We run talks, workshops, reading groups, and social events so people working on similar topics can meet, share ideas, and collaborate.

Part of Utrecht NLP Hub

The Young Scholar Network operates as an integral part of the Utrecht NLP Hub, one of the leading research centers for Natural Language Processing in the Netherlands.

The Utrecht NLP Hub brings together researchers from Utrecht University and University Medical Center Utrecht (UMCU), fostering a long-standing tradition of excellence in linguistics, computational research, and clinical NLP applications.

Our network benefits from this collaborative environment, providing access to world-class facilities, diverse research expertise spanning both academic and clinical domains, and strong international connections.
