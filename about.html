About YSN

Building a thriving community for the next generation of NLP researchers

Our Mission

The Young Scholar Network (YSN) at Utrecht NLP Hub is dedicated to creating a supportive and collaborative environment for early-career researchers in Natural Language Processing.

We believe that the next generation of NLP researchers deserves a platform where they can share ideas, develop skills, and build meaningful connections that will shape the future of the field.

Through our network, we aim to bridge the gap between academic research and practical application, fostering innovation and excellence in NLP research.

Our Vision

Shaping the future of NLP research through community, collaboration, and innovation

Excellence in Research

We strive to maintain the highest standards of research quality and integrity, encouraging rigorous methodology and innovative approaches.

Global Collaboration

We promote international collaboration and knowledge exchange, connecting Utrecht's research community with scholars worldwide.

Inclusive Community

We champion diversity and inclusion, ensuring all voices are heard and valued in our community regardless of background or experience level.

Sustainable Impact

We focus on creating lasting impact through responsible AI research and ethical considerations in NLP development.

Our Goals

What we aim to achieve together

01

Foster Research Collaboration

Create opportunities for interdisciplinary collaboration through joint projects, reading groups, and research partnerships that bridge different areas of NLP.

02

Develop Professional Skills

Provide workshops and training sessions on essential skills including academic writing, presentation techniques, grant writing, and research methodology.

03

Build Community Connections

Organize regular networking events, social gatherings, and informal meetups to strengthen bonds within the NLP research community.

04

Share Knowledge & Resources

Maintain a comprehensive repository of research tools, datasets, papers, and best practices accessible to all network members.

05

Support Career Development

Facilitate mentorship programs, career guidance sessions, and connections with industry partners to support diverse career paths.

06

Promote Open Science

Encourage open research practices, reproducibility, and transparent communication of research findings to the broader community.

Part of Utrecht NLP Hub

The Young Scholar Network operates as an integral part of the Utrecht NLP Hub, one of the leading research centers for Natural Language Processing in the Netherlands.

The Utrecht NLP Hub brings together researchers from Utrecht University and University Medical Center Utrecht (UMCU), fostering a long-standing tradition of excellence in linguistics, computational research, and clinical NLP applications.

Our network benefits from this collaborative environment, providing access to world-class facilities, diverse research expertise spanning both academic and clinical domains, and strong international connections.
