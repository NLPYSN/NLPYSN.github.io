Our Mission

Building a supportive community for young NLP researchers

Community

Foster a collaborative environment where early-career researchers can connect, share ideas, and support each other's academic journey.

Learning

Provide opportunities for skill development through workshops, seminars, and knowledge-sharing sessions led by experts in the field.

Research

Promote cutting-edge research in NLP by facilitating collaborations, sharing resources, and showcasing innovative work.

Growth

Support career development through mentorship programs, networking events, and professional development opportunities.

What We Offer

Comprehensive support for your research journey

01

Regular Events

Monthly seminars, workshops, and reading groups covering the latest developments in NLP research and methodology.

02

Networking

Connect with peers, senior researchers, and industry professionals through our vibrant community events.

03

Resources

Access curated collections of papers, tools, datasets, and best practices for NLP research.

04

Mentorship

Benefit from guidance by experienced researchers who understand the challenges of early-career academia.
